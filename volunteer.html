VOLUNTEER

For a fun and rewarding experience, help make a difference by volunteering with Starlight! By committing to at least two shifts a month you will instantly feel part of the Starlight family and we can guarantee you will love every moment! If you are a fun, positive and reliable person we want you to join us in running our incredible programs.

Learn more about our incredible initiatives:

By taking part in Wishgranting you will help realise an ill child’s dream and enable them to have a once-in-a-lifetime experience. Since no wish is the same, this volunteer opportunity will have you taking part in a wide range of tasks. These could be helping a child brainstorm their wish, navigating an airport, delivering gifts or taking children and their families on an excursion.

Taking the role of Captain Starlight’s helpers, in the Starlight Express Rooms you will provide fun activities for our kids and their families to participate in. These could be crafts, movies, trivia, dancing or games all with the main objective to bring a smile to every child’s face and help them forget about the hospital and their treatments.

As Livewire is targeted at teenagers, you will create interesting workshops for this age group. These could focus on topics like music, film making, creative writing and art in order to enhance confidence and self-esteem, and engage them in fun, learning experiences that provide them with a sense of normalcy.
